Ramon Soriano can tell you exactly how many tacos La Calle Tacos has sold in ten years: 4 million. He can also tell you those tacos weigh roughly 750 tons, that stacked an inch thick they would rise about 63 miles into the air, and lined up end to end they would stretch around 315 miles.
